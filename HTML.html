About Us

Our Story

Savora LEAF Exports LTD is a premier exporter of premium tea leaves and coffee seeds based in Kolkata, India. With decades of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted supplier to markets in the US, UK, and Middle East.

Our commitment to quality begins at the source. We work directly with select tea gardens and coffee plantations across India to ensure that only the finest products reach our international customers. Our rigorous quality control processes and sustainable farming practices set us apart in the global market.

From the CEO's Desk

"At Savora LEAF, we believe in building lasting relationships with our clients through trust, quality, and consistency. Our mission is to bring the authentic taste of Indian tea and coffee to the world while supporting sustainable agricultural practices."

CEO & Founder

Our Premium Products

Premium Coffee Seeds

Our coffee seeds are sourced from the finest plantations in India, including regions like Coorg, Chikmagalur, and the Nilgiris. We offer various grades and roasts to meet the specific requirements of our international clients.

Available varieties include Arabica, Robusta, and specialty micro-lot coffees with distinctive flavor profiles ranging from chocolatey and nutty to fruity and floral notes.

Exquisite Tea Leaves

Our tea collection features the finest leaves from Darjeeling, Assam, and Nilgiri regions. Each batch is carefully selected and processed to preserve its unique characteristics and flavor profile.

We offer black, green, white, and specialty teas including first flush Darjeeling, robust Assam, and aromatic blends crafted to perfection for discerning international markets.
